# **App Name**: Calabar FuelFinder

## Core Features:

- Live Fuel Prices Display: View an alphabetical list of up to 5 filling stations on the homepage, displaying the actual price per litre for both petrol and diesel. Recently viewed and favourited stations will also be prominently featured.
- Station Search & Location: Search for specific filling stations by name. The app will prioritize displaying the closest station of that company to your current location.
- Interactive Map Integration: View the locations of filling stations on an interactive map powered by Google Maps.
- Fuel Price Calculator: Calculate the approximate litres of fuel or diesel obtainable for a specified amount of money.
- Price Increment Notifications: Receive push notifications for upcoming general fuel price increment announcements.
- Company Feedback System: A comment box allowing users to submit feedback directly to the company.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A bright burnt orange (e.g., #D86B2F) for key interactive elements, conveying energy and approachability.
- Secondary color: A calming sea green (e.g., #2F8A7A) for secondary elements, accents, and information hierarchy.
- Background color: A very light, peachy cream (#FCF6F2), providing a clean and inviting canvas.
- Headlines and Body text font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for its modern, neutral, and highly legible characteristics, suitable for data-heavy content and news.
- Use clear, concise, and modern line-art icons that are universally recognizable, such as a fuel pump for stations, a magnifying glass for search, and a bell for notifications. Favor filled icon styles for improved visibility.
- Implement a clean, card-based layout for fuel station listings on the homepage, prioritizing critical information. Employ intuitive navigation with clearly labeled sections for map, news, and calculator, ensuring a mobile-first responsive design.
- Subtle, fluid animations for page transitions and data loading indicators to enhance user experience without being distracting. Focus on conveying responsiveness and dynamic content updates.
